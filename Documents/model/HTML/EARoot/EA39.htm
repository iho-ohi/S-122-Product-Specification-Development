An abstract superclass for information types that encode rules, recommendations, and general information in text or graphic form.<br/>Remarks: Subtypes of AbstractRxN carry the same attributes, but differ in the nature of information they encode. There are currently four such subtypes: Regulations, Restrictions, Recommendations, and NauticalInformation.<br/>
